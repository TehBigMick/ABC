About Bella Andre

A romance author with broad reader appeal, strong series branding, and very easy entry points for new readers.

Bella Andre writes bestselling contemporary romance with warmth, glamour, chemistry, and emotional payoff. Her books are built for readers who want to settle into a romantic world and keep going.

For new readers, Married in Malibu is a smooth starting point if weddings and California romance appeal more. The Maverick Billionaires is the stronger hook if you want richer fantasy, bigger stakes, and billionaire heroes.

Either way, this is the kind of catalogue that rewards series reading rather than one-and-done browsing.
