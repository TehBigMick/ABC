About Penny

Penny Reid is a New York Times, Wall Street Journal and USA Today bestselling author. She previously wrote federal grant proposals as a biomedical researcher before turning to romance fiction. Today she writes self‑published “kissing books”, manages the Smartypants Romance imprint and lives in Seattle with her husband, three children and a dog【206033809642826†L53-L59】. Reid is widely recognised as a self‑published success; she was the first self‑published author invited on the Professional Book Nerds podcast【906918119404983†L51-L62】.
