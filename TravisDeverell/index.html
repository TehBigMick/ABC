About Travis Deverell

Serial-fiction roots, major-series payoff.

Travis Deverell writes as Shirtaloon. What began in online serialised fiction grew into one of the best-known names in modern LitRPG and progression fantasy.

The commercial appeal is easy to see: high magic, wild adventure, absurd humour, and a series built for readers who want more than one book to disappear into.

This page is not trying to make the brand feel more literary or more solemn than it is. That would be the wrong pitch. The strength here is that the books are fun, confident, and built to carry momentum across a series.
