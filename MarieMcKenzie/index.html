About Marie

Marie McKenzie is a registered nurse, educator, and award-winning #1 Amazon bestselling author whose work centres on trauma-informed healing.

With over two decades of healthcare experience, she writes with clinical insight and human depth — offering language that feels calm, steady, and safe.

Rooted in dignity, choice, and compassion, Marie believes healing happens best when people feel respected — never pressured. Readers often describe her books as grounding and practical.
