Life Skills & Confidence Books for Teens and Tweens

Tory Hunt writes practical, motivating guides for teens, tweens and teenage boys who want to feel more capable and prepared for adulthood. Her books focus on real-world skills young people can apply immediately — responsibility, money basics, independence and stronger habits.

Parents searching for life skills books for teenage boys or adulting books for teens want something modern and actionable. Tory’s approach avoids fluff and lectures. Instead, her guides deliver clear steps that help build confidence through consistent action.

About Tory

Tory Hunt is an entrepreneur and writer with a passion for helping teens build confidence, life skills and independence. As a mother of four, she understands the pressures young people face today — identity, money decisions and growing responsibility.

Her mission is simple: create clear, practical resources that support teens and tweens as they develop habits and skills they can carry into adulthood.
