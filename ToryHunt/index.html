Life Skills & Confidence Books for Teens, Tweens and Teenage Boys

Tory Hunt writes practical, motivating guides for young people who want to feel more confident, capable and prepared for adult life. Her books focus on the real-world skills teenagers and tweens can use immediately: independence, responsibility, money basics, better habits and stronger decision-making. The goal is simple, help young readers build direction without feeling overwhelmed.

Parents often search for life skills books for teenage boys or “adulting” books for teens because they want something clear, modern and genuinely useful. Tory’s approach stays grounded: practical steps, relatable language and tools that teens will actually apply. Whether it’s navigating pressure, identity, school stress or money decisions, small wins build momentum, and momentum builds confidence.

These books are designed to fit into everyday life: at home, at school and during the transition into independence. If you’re looking for a teen life skills book that supports responsibility, confidence and a stronger sense of direction, Tory’s guides are built to deliver quick wins and lasting habits.

About Tory

Tory Hunt is an entrepreneur and writer with a passion for helping teens build confidence, life skills, and a strong sense of direction. As a devoted mother of four, she understands the real challenges young people face today, pressure, identity, money decisions, and growing independence, and she’s committed to making those years easier to navigate.

Tory’s work in the teen niche grew from a simple goal: create practical, encouraging resources that speak to teens in a clear, relatable way. Through engaging guides and tools, she helps young readers develop habits and skills they can carry into adulthood.

When she’s not working, Tory loves exploring new places and cultures, always chasing the next experience. She’s also an avid reader who enjoys learning, finding inspiration, and escaping into great stories. Most of all, she treasures family time and the memories that keep her grounded and motivated.
