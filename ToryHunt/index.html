Life Skills & Confidence Books for Teens and Teenage Boys

Tory Hunt writes modern, practical guides for teens who want to feel more confident, capable and prepared for adult life. Her books focus on real-world skills teenagers can use immediately: building better habits, improving decision-making, developing independence and learning responsibility without feeling overwhelmed.

Parents often search for life skills books for teenage boys because they want something clear, motivating and actually useful — not lectures, not fluff, and not content that feels childish. Tory’s approach is straight to the point: practical steps, relatable examples and simple tools teens can apply in everyday life at home, at school and beyond.

Whether a teen is navigating identity, pressure, confidence issues or money decisions, the goal is the same: create momentum. Small wins build self-belief. Consistent habits build direction. This is what Tory’s teen guides are designed to support — a stronger mindset, stronger independence and more control over the choices that shape adulthood.

About Tory

Tory Hunt is an entrepreneur and writer with a passion for helping teens build confidence, life skills, and a strong sense of direction. As a devoted mother of four, she understands the real challenges young people face today—pressure, identity, money decisions, and growing independence—and she’s committed to making those years easier to navigate.

Tory’s work in the teen niche grew from a simple goal: create practical, encouraging resources that speak to teens in a clear, relatable way. Through engaging guides and tools, she helps young readers develop habits and skills they can carry into adulthood.

When she’s not working, Tory loves exploring new places and cultures, always chasing the next experience. She’s also an avid reader who enjoys learning, finding inspiration, and escaping into great stories. Most of all, she treasures family time and the memories that keep her grounded and motivated.
