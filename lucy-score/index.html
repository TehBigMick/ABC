About Bella

Bella Andre is a New York Times, USA Today and international bestselling author whose success helped redefine independent publishing. After feeling frustrated with traditional publishing’s lack of control over cover design and marketing, she regained the rights to her earlier books and independently published Take Me through Amazon’s Kindle program in March 2010【772715766593467†L166-L173】. She taught herself to format the ebook and design its cover. Later that year she self‑published Love Me and Game for Love; the latter reached the Barnes & Noble digital top 25【772715766593467†L179-L206】. Bella’s pioneering approach earned her the label of “one of the hottest digital writers in America” and allowed her to build The Sullivans series, which in 2013 had five titles simultaneously on the New York Times and USA Today bestseller lists【772715766593467†L241-L242】.
