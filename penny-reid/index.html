About Lucy

Lucy Score is a self‑made phenomenon and New York Times #1 bestselling author. After self‑publishing a novella that sold only 35 copies, two indie authors encouraged her to expand it into a full‑length novel. In March 2015 Undercover Love was released, followed later that year by Pretend You’re Mine which hit #1 on Amazon and allowed her to become a full‑time writer【457560129025851†L75-L103】. She wrote several more books and in 2017 she and her husband decided to officially become indie publishers【457560129025851†L134-L151】. Her romances blend laugh‑out‑loud humour, swoon‑worthy love stories and memorable small‑town communities.
