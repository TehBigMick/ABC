About Penny Reid

A bestselling romance author with a science background, which makes this trilogy feel especially on-brand.

Penny Reid is a New York Times, Wall Street Journal, and USA Today bestselling author best known for the Winston Brothers, Knitting in the City, and other smart romance series.

Before writing fiction full time, she worked as a biomedical researcher writing federal grant proposals, which gives the science-centred edge of Fundamentals of Biology real credibility as well as charm.

Her books are known for wit, intelligence, emotional payoff, and characters who feel sharper than the average rom-com leads.
