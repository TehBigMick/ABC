About Matt Dinniman

Writer, artist, and very clearly not interested in sounding tidy.

Matt Dinniman is a writer and artist from Gig Harbor, Washington, and the author of the NYT bestselling Dungeon Crawler Carl series.

He also writes across horror, fantasy, sci-fi thriller, and LitRPG, which is why his catalogue does not feel like one neat shelf. It feels like a man with too many bad ideas and the skill to make all of them work.

That is the appeal. The voice is punchy, strange, and fully committed. Even when the books are funny, horror is still in the bones.
