Why this page exists

A better way into Amy Cross

Amy Cross has a deep horror catalogue, which is exactly why new readers can bounce off if they are left to figure it out alone. This page fixes that. It gives you a clean way in: one strong first read, one bingeable series path, and a few supporting books that show the range without drowning you in choice.

The focus here is simple: haunted houses, ghost-haunted places, sinister towns and sharp, atmospheric horror that keeps you clicking for the next one.
