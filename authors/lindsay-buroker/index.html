Why this style matches the books

You’re selling an *experience*. The page should feel like it belongs to the genre.

  • Parchment tones + serif headings = “bookish” without being tacky
  • Gold/emerald accents hint at fantasy without screaming “clip art dragon”
  • Featured shelf keeps choice overload under control
  • Buy buttons remain the loudest action on the page

How AuthorsBookClub would build the full version

  • Series sections with reading order
  • “Start here” guidance pinned at the top
  • Retailer buttons for Amazon/Kobo/Apple Books (author choice)
  • Optional mailing list / social links block
