About Marie

Marie McKenzie is a registered nurse, educator, and award-winning #1 Amazon bestselling author whose work centres on trauma-informed healing.

With over two decades of healthcare experience, she writes with clinical insight and human depth — but what readers often notice first is the tone: calm, honest, and steady. Her books do not rush healing or promise quick transformation. Instead, they offer clarity and language for those navigating difficult seasons.

Rooted in dignity, choice, and compassion, Marie believes healing happens best when people feel respected — never pressured to explain or perform their pain. Readers often describe her books as grounding — something to return to when life feels heavy or unclear.
